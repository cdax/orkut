AND another thing...
For those of us who have no clue (yet?!) about the Pascal Triangle, here's the deal:

The terms of the nth line of the Pascal Triangle, are the co-efficients of x^i in the expansion of (x+1)^n, where 0<=i<=n.

e.g., the 4th line of the Triangle will be:

1 4 6 4 1

since the expansion of (x+1)^n is:

1.x^4 + 4.x^3 + 6.x^2 + 4.x^1 + 1.x^0
