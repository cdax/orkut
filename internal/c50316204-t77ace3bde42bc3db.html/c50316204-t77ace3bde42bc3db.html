Chitharanjan! Das - 30 de setembro de 2008
As a matter of fact....
.... whatever you write within the brackets of PrintVarName (after the #define directive, i.e.) will not matter, as long as it is a single word... e.g.,

#define PrintVarName(x) printf(#x)

could have as easily been replaced by :
#define PrintVarName(int) printf(#int)
OR
#define PrintVarName(intx) printf(#intx)
